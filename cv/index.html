Professional Summary

Translational neuroscientist with over a decade of experience bridging molecular biomarker assay development with mechanistic disease biology across Parkinson’s disease, Alzheimer’s disease, and pediatric epilepsy. First- or co-first-author on 14 peer-reviewed research articles spanning α-synuclein proteostasis, amyloid-β aggregation, and translational nanotherapeutics. Currently expanding into iPSC-derived organoid models, HD-MEA electrophysiology, and 2PP-fabricated implantable microdevices for localized brain delivery.
