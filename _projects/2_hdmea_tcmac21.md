## Status: current / in-development

Acute-slice **HD-MEA electrophysiology** on 6–7-week-old female TcMAC21 (Tc(HSA21, CAG-EGFP)1Yakaz/J) transgenic mice and wild-type littermates, recorded on **MaxOne** MEA chips (MaxWell Biosystems). All animal procedures are conducted at the Children's National Research Animal Facility under an IACUC-approved protocol.

**Readouts.** Spike firing-rate mapping, inter-spike-interval (ISI) distributions, spike-amplitude histograms, and pharmacological probes of network excitability. Data are analyzed with MaxLab Live / MaxLive and complemented by custom Python analysis (kernel density estimation, spike-train summarization).

**Complementary published thread.** This ongoing electrophysiological work sits alongside a recently published characterization of disrupted neurogenesis from basal intermediate precursor cells in the postnatal *TcMAC21* neocortex (Atak, Srivastava, et al., *J. Neuroscience*, 2025).

*Ongoing electrophysiological work. Circuit-level findings are not yet peer-reviewed.*
