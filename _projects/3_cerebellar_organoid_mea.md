## Status: current / in-development

Acute and longitudinal MEA recording of iPSC-derived cerebellar organoids on day 63, using **MaxOne PLM** and **PSM** multielectrode-array chips (MaxWell Biosystems). Prior to plating, MEA chips are sterilized, preconditioned, and coated with poly-D-lysine (0.1 mg mL⁻¹) and laminin (0.04 mg mL⁻¹) to support tissue adhesion. Organoids are cultured in BrainPhys medium supplemented with N2 Supplement-A and NeuroCult SM1 before being placed onto the chip.

**Readouts.** Spatial firing-rate mapping (silent < 5 Hz, slow 5–50 Hz, fast > 50 Hz), spike-amplitude and ISI distributions, and kernel-density modelling of population firing structure — all rendered by a custom Python visualization pipeline.

*This organoid-MEA thread is in active development; results are not yet peer-reviewed.*
