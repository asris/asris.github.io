KCNN2/SK2 immunoblotting in postmortem FASD brain

Sequential cytosolic and membrane-enriched fractionation with KCNN2/SK2 immunoblotting on postmortem human brain from control and FASD donors.

Status: current / in-development

Sequential cytosolic and membrane-enriched protein extraction using the Mem-PER Plus kit (Thermo Fisher, Cat. 89842) with protease and phosphatase inhibitors, followed by BCA quantitation (Pierce BCA, Cat. 23225) and Western / JESS capillary immunoblotting for KCNN2 (SK2) in control and fetal alcohol spectrum disorder (FASD) postmortem human brain.

Tissue sources. Brain Endowment Bank, Miller School of Medicine, University of Miami · NIH NeuroBioBank at the University of Pittsburgh · University of Maryland Brain and Tissue Bank. All work is conducted under the applicable biobank consent, material-transfer, and institutional ethics approvals. Donor metadata (age, sex, postmortem interval, brain region, diagnosis, handling variables) are curated as a supplementary table.

Ongoing postmortem biochemistry work; the analysis is not yet peer-reviewed.
